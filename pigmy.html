About Us

Official Image:

MISSION:-

  • To meet and serve credit requirements of trade and business initiatives.
  • To extend a supporting hand for development of small scale business organisations.
  • To inculcate a habbit of saving money a wise manner to all traders/entrepreneurs/small scale businessmen and profiting them by giving back their saving back with profit(interest) at the time of investments.
  • Also to extend loans to them at the time of investments and to overcome loses.

VISION:-

  • To establish our branches and extend our service in all areas of need all across the state.
  • To totally contribute towards Rural Credit and Trade Credit.
  • Vidhaatru Nidhi Limited should become the first choiceof every traders/entrepreneurs/small scale businessmen in their investments and savings.
  • To provide employment opportunities to the needy.
